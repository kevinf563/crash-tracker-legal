# Support Page for Plane and Train Crash Tracker

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### General Questions

#### What is Plane and Train Crash Tracker?
Plane and Train Crash Tracker is a mobile app that provides real-time notifications about aviation and railway incidents worldwide. We aggregate verified news from trusted sources to keep you informed about transportation safety.

#### How much does it cost?
- **Free Trial**: 3 days, full access to all features
- **Subscription**: $4.99/month after trial
- **No hidden fees**: Cancel anytime

#### How do I start my free trial?
1. Download the app from the App Store
2. Open the app
3. Tap "Start Free Trial"
4. No credit card required - billing starts after 3 days

#### How do I cancel my subscription?
1. Open iOS **Settings**
2. Tap your **Apple ID** (top of screen)
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Select **Plane and Train Crash Tracker**
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**

Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period.

### Notifications

#### I'm not receiving notifications. What should I do?

**Step 1: Check App Permissions**
1. Open iOS **Settings**
2. Scroll to **Plane and Train Crash Tracker**
3. Tap **Notifications**
4. Ensure **Allow Notifications** is ON

**Step 2: Check In-App Settings**
1. Open the app
2. Go to **Notifications** tab
3. Ensure notification types are enabled
4. Try **"Send Test Notification"** button

**Step 3: Check Do Not Disturb**
- Swipe down from top-right (Control Center)
- Ensure Focus/Do Not Disturb is off

**Step 4: Restart Device**
- Sometimes iOS needs a restart to refresh notification system

#### Can I customize notification sounds?
Currently, we use the default notification sound. Custom sounds may be added in future updates!

#### How many notifications will I receive?
This depends on real-world incident occurrence. On average:
- **High activity day**: 5-10 notifications
- **Normal day**: 2-5 notifications
- **Low activity day**: 0-2 notifications

You can reduce notifications by enabling country filters.

#### Can I get notifications only for my country?
Yes!
1. Go to **Notifications** tab
2. Tap **Filter by Country**
3. Select your country
4. You'll only receive notifications for that country

Not currently, but we're considering family sharing for future updates!

### Technical Issues

#### The app is crashing. What should I do?
1. **Update iOS**: Ensure you have latest iOS version
2. **Update App**: Check App Store for updates
3. **Restart Device**: Turn off and on again
4. **Reinstall App**: Delete and reinstall from App Store
5. **Contact Support**: If issue persists, email us

#### Articles won't open. How do I fix this?
1. **Check Internet**: Ensure you have WiFi or cellular data
2. **Safari Permissions**: Ensure Safari is not restricted in Screen Time settings
3. **Try Again**: Some news sites may be temporarily down
4. **Update App**: Check for app updates

#### Data isn't loading. What's wrong?
- **Check Internet Connection**: WiFi or cellular data required
- **Pull to Refresh**: Swipe down on incident list
- **Restart App**: Close completely and reopen
- **Check API Status**: Rarely, our news provider may have issues

#### The app is slow or laggy.
1. **Close Background Apps**: Double-click home, swipe up on other apps
2. **Restart Device**: Often fixes performance issues
3. **Free Up Storage**: Ensure you have at least 1GB free space
4. **Update iOS**: Latest iOS versions have performance improvements

#### I lost my data after reinstalling. Can I recover it?
Your notification preferences are stored locally. If you reinstall:
- Subscription status: Automatically restored
- Notification preferences: Need to be set again
- Viewed incidents: Will reload from news sources

#### What data do you collect?
We collect minimal data:

- Usage statistics (anonymous)

We DO NOT collect:
- Personal identification information

#### Is my data shared with third parties?

#### How do I delete my account and data?
1. Delete the app from your device
2. Cancel your subscription (see above)

#### Is the app secure?
Yes:
- ✅ All communications encrypted (HTTPS)
- ✅ No sensitive data stored
- ✅ Apple's secure payment system
- ✅ Regular security updates

### Troubleshooting Guides

#### Complete Reset Guide
If you're experiencing persistent issues:

1. **Note Your Preferences**: Write down your notification settings
2. **Cancel Subscription** (if you want to stop billing)
3. **Delete App**: Hold app icon → Remove App
4. **Restart Device**: Full power off and on
5. **Reinstall**: Download fresh from App Store
6. **Reconfigure**: Set up your preferences again

Your subscription will automatically restore.

#### Notification Test Checklist
Not getting notifications? Try these in order:

- [ ] App notifications enabled in iOS Settings
- [ ] At least one incident type enabled (plane/train)
- [ ] If using country filter, at least one country selected
- [ ] Do Not Disturb is off
- [ ] Device has internet connection
- [ ] App is updated to latest version
- [ ] Device has been restarted recently
- [ ] Test notification works (Notifications tab → Send Test)
